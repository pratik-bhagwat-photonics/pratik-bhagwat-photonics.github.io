I’m Pratik Bhagwat, an experimental physicist working in photophysics and optical spectroscopy. My academic background spans physics, materials research, and photonics, beginning at Savitribai Phule Pune University in India and continuing at Lund University in Sweden. Throughout this journey, my interests have gradually shifted from studying materials to using optical experiments to investigate the photophysical processes.

---

## 🎓 Research Development
My early research focused on nanomaterials, electromagnetic phenomena, and materials characterization. While this provided a strong experimental foundation, I became increasingly interested in understanding how optical measurements could reveal the underlying physics of materials. This led me to pursue photonics, where my master's research centred on ultrafast transient absorption spectroscopy to investigate exciton and spin dynamics in chiral two-dimensional lead-halide perovskites.
